Largest city and Capital City in Alberta

The largest city in Alberta is Calgary,Alberta while the capital city in Alberta is Edmonton,Alberta. First, Calgary is the city that has the largest population in Alberta and is the 4th largest metropolitan area in Canada. It is located inside the transition zone that seperates the Canadian Rockies from the Canadian Prairies (Western Alberta) and is over 3000 feet above sea level. The current mayor of Calgary is Naheed Nenshi. Second, Edmonton is the city that has the second largest population in Alberta and is the 6th largest metropolitan area in Canada. Edmonton is located in Central Alberta near the North Saskatchewan river and is over 2000 feet above sea level. The current mayor of Edmonton is Don Iverson. Calgary's tourist attractions include the Calgary Zoo, Heritage Park Historical Village, and Fort Calgary while Edmonton's tourist attractions include West Edmonton Mall, Muttart Conservatory, and Telus World of Science.

Professional sports teams in Alberta

The professional sports teams in Alberta include the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League (NHL), the Calgary Flames of the National Hockey League (NHL), the Edmonton Eskimos of the Canadian Football League (CFL), and the Calgary Stampeders of the Canadian Football League (CFL).

Interesting facts about Alberta

1. The halloween phrase "Trick or Treat" originated in Alberta in 1927.

2. The first Boston Pizza restaurant was opened in Edmonton, Alberta.

3. The biggest beaver dam in the world is in Wood Buffalo National Park in Northern Alberta

4. West Edmonton Mall is the biggest mall in North America.

5. Alberta is the province that produces the most coal in Canada.
